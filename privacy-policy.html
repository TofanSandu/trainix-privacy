How We Use Your Information

We use your information to operate and improve Vortrex, including:

Data Storage (Supabase)

We use Supabase to handle authentication and store user-generated data (e.g., profiles, routines, logs). Supabase acts as our data processor under their terms. Access to data is restricted and secured.

AI Services (DeepSeek API)

We use the DeepSeek API to assist with workout and coaching content generation. Prompts may include your fitness goals and relevant context to produce responses. We do not share unnecessary personal data in prompts.

Payments and Subscriptions

Purchases are handled by Apple App Store and Google Play billing through RevenueCat. We do not store full payment details. RevenueCat provides subscription status and entitlement information to unlock features in the app.
