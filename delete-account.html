Delete Your Account & Data

If you want to delete your Vortrex (Trainix) account and associated data, email us and we will process your request.

How to request deletion

  1. Email trainix2@gmail.com from the same email used in the app.
  2. Use subject: Delete my account.
  3. Include: “Delete my account and all associated data.”

What we delete

  • Account and profile data (Supabase)
  • Workout routines, history, nutrition logs
  • Subscription metadata (RevenueCat)

What may be retained

  • Legally required billing records from Google Play/RevenueCat

Timeline

We aim to complete deletion within 30 days of verifying your request. This is irreversible.

If you have questions, contact trainix2@gmail.com.
