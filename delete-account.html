Delete Your Account & Data

If you want to delete your Vortrex (Trainix) account and associated data, follow the steps below.

How to request deletion

  1. Email trainix2@gmail.com from the same email you use in the app.
  2. Subject: Delete my account.
  3. Include this text: “Please delete my account and all associated data.”

What we delete

  • Account and profile data (Supabase)
  • Workout routines, history, and nutrition logs
  • Subscription metadata (RevenueCat)

What may be retained

  • Legally required billing records from Google Play/RevenueCat

Timeline

We aim to complete deletion within 30 days of verifying your request. This is irreversible.

Questions? Contact trainix2@gmail.com.
